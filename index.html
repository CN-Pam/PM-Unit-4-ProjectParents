Maritza & Nelson Martinez

Who they are?

They've been married for 32 years & met in the Dominican Republic in their 20s. They had a long distance relationship for 4 years after they married because Nelson lived in NY & Maritza lived in DR with their kids. Eventually, they all joined Nelson in Brooklyn.

They LOVE to dance. This is their favorite song to dance to together!

Why they inspire me?

My parents inspire me because of how hard they have worked to take care of my sisters & I. They have worked harder than ANYONE I know. This showed my sisters & I how important it is to get an education so we wouldn't have to work as hard later in life.

What they do?

My parents own a Brooklyn bodega. Year round, you'll find flowers, Christmas tress, Pumpkins or our cat Mars. They are open 7 days a week & have become a staple of Carroll Gardens.
